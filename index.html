about

My name is Jack Replinger and I am a teacher and entrepreneur. I came to Memphis in 2007 with Teacher For America and have taught for 12 years. I currently Teach and currently teach at The Soulsville Charter School. I am the founder, co-developer and UX designer for Positive Physics.
  • 2015 Charter School Teacher of the Year
  • 2018 TFA Alumni Leadership Award
  • Featured in Chalkbeat and Daily Memphian Article

projects
